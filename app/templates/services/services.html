Our Services

What We Offer

More Than Just Products

Our services are designed to support farmers at every stage of the growing season — from soil preparation through to post-harvest advice.

Soil Testing & Analysis

Understanding your soil is the foundation of a successful crop. Our certified lab provides comprehensive soil testing and actionable fertilizer recommendations.

  • Soil pH, macronutrient and micronutrient analysis
  • Organic matter and cation exchange capacity testing
  • Written fertilizer prescription for your specific crop
  • Follow-up agronomist consultation included
  • Results within 5–7 working days

Agronomic Advisory

Our team of certified agronomists provides personalised, on-farm consultancy to help you get the most from your land and investment.

  • Farm visits and crop monitoring throughout the season
  • Integrated pest and disease management planning
  • Fertilizer programme development and review
  • Irrigation and water management advice
  • Crop budgeting and input planning support

Bulk Supply & Contracts

AgroChemix is a trusted partner for commercial farms, farmer cooperatives, NGOs, and government agricultural programmes requiring reliable bulk input supply.

  • Competitive pricing on bulk orders of 5+ tonnes
  • Supply contracts for the full growing season
  • Warehousing and phased delivery available
  • Documentation support for tender requirements
  • FIFO stock management and quality guarantees

Spray & Application Services

Not every farmer has the equipment or expertise for precise chemical application. Our certified operators ensure safe, effective results.

  • Herbicide, pesticide and foliar application services
  • Calibrated spray equipment for uniform coverage
  • Operator safety and PPE compliance guaranteed
  • Fertilizer broadcasting and topdressing services
  • Available for smallholder and commercial scale

Input Credit & Financing

Access to inputs shouldn't be limited by cash flow. We've partnered with financial institutions to provide flexible input financing for qualifying farmers.

  • Seasonal input credit for registered farmers
  • Crop-linked repayment through harvest proceeds
  • Group-based lending for cooperatives and clubs
  • Simple application process with fast approval
